 \ About

Future Humans Bristol is a forum for researchers whose work focuses on the relationship between human and technological interaction.

Future Humans Bristol aims to become a cohort - working together to answer the biggest technological questions facing our future.

The Future Humans Workshop 2017 will be a chance for postgraduate researchers from across the University of Bristol to come together to discuss, create, and collaborate on the question of:

'What is the future for the relationship between humans and technology?'.

After the workshop, Future Humans Bristol hopes to become an academic collective of shared expertise from across the University of Bristol.

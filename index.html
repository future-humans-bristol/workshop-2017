About

The Future Humans Workshop is a unique opportunity for Bristol postgraduate researchers from across the University to come together, share skills and ideas, and collaborate on the topic of humanity's future relationship with technology.

What is the future for the relationship between humans and technology?

Workshop 2017

AI for the home / Prosthetics / Cultural evolution/ Driverless cars / Living with technology / Tactile robotics / Orthopaedic implants / Transhumanism / Virtual reality / Drug delivery / Legal challenges / Impact on infrastructure

The list goes on.

If your research involves thinking about how the future of technology will shape us, or how our future will influence technology, this workshop is designed for you.

The aim of this workshop is to be inspired, share ideas, and collaborate with other researchers on the theme. Collaborators will be given the opportunity to deliver prsentations at the Univeristy of Bristol Postgraduate Reserach Festival in May.

About

The Future Humans research workshop is a unique opportunity for Bristol postgraduate researchers from across the University to collaborate on the topic of humanity's future relationship with technology.

How will technology shape our society, our bodies, and our culture?

AI for the home / Prosthetics / Cultural evolution / Driverless cars / Living with technology / Tactile robotics / Orthopaedic implants / Transhumanism / Virtual reality / Drug delivery / Legal challenges / Impact on infrastructure

The list goes on.

Workshop 2017

If your research involves thinking about how the future of technology will shape us, or how our future will influence technology, this workshop is aimed at you.

The Future Humans research workshop is a one-day event designed to let participants develop their ideas with others and help shape them.

There will be specially designed exercises, prompts, and space for you to turn your ideas into mini projects. The day will provide the opportunity to refine and present your PhD ideas to a new crowd, and make new connections.

Be inspired, share ideas, and collaborate with other researchers on the future for humans and the technology they create.
